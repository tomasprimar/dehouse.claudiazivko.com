ESTABLIMENTS, MALLORCA

DE HOUSE

LOCATION

Establiments is a quiet village on the northern edge of Palma, tucked into the foothills of the Serra de Tramuntana — the limestone range that shelters the island's western coast and has been recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage landscape.

From the house it is a ten-minute drive to the centre of Palma and its old town, fifteen to the port, and under twenty to the airport. Nearby are the island's most reputed golf courses, the hiking trails of Valldemossa and Bunyola, and the beaches of the Bay of Palma.
